Quick Explanations

Format: A format in Magic: The Gathering determines which cards are eligible for play. For example, Pauper restricts decks to only common rarity cards, while Legacy permits the use of any cards printed since the game's inception. Each format has its own rules and restrictions, shaping the types of strategies and decks that can be used in gameplay.

Arechtype: An archetype defines the overarching strategy of a deck. Common archetypes include Aggro, Control, Combo, Tempo, and Midrange, each representing distinct approaches to gameplay and card selection. Understanding these archetypes helps players strategize effectively and build decks that align with their preferred playstyle.
