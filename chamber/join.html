Membership Level Information

Nonprofit Membership Level

Nonprofit Membership Level

The Nonprofit Membership Level is a complimentary option that allows us to promote your business through all our promotional materials and on our directory website. Additionally, your business will receive a dedicated stand at every promotional event hosted by the Commerce, providing valuable exposure and networking opportunities.

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership Level

The Bronze Membership Level is a complimentary option for for-profit businesses, enabling us to showcase your company in our promotional materials and on our businesses directory. You will have the opportunity to set up a stand at any event you choose to participate in, maximizing your visibility and engagement.

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership Level

The Silver Membership Level is a paid option for for-profit businesses, available for just $10 per month. This membership allows for your business to be prominently advertised in our promotional materials and on our website. In addition, you will receive a dedicated stand at all events, enhancing your visibility and opportunities for engagement.
